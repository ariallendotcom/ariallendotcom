Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ari Allen ("we," "I") handles information in connection with a personal automated SMS reminder service operated through ariallen.com.

Overview

This is a personal messaging service. It sends automated reminder and daily check-in text messages from the operator to the operator's own mobile phone number. It is not a commercial service and does not message the general public.

Information We Use

The only personal information used is the operator's own mobile phone number, used solely to deliver these reminder messages, and the content of the operator's own check-in replies, used solely to maintain the operator's personal records.

No Sharing With Third Parties

No mobile information, opt-in data, or consent is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This information is never sold, rented, or shared with anyone.

SMS Consent & Messaging

By enabling this service, the operator consents to receive automated text messages at the number provided. Message frequency varies (typically up to two messages per day). Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Security

The phone number and message content are stored privately and used only to operate this personal service.

Changes

This policy may be updated; the "last updated" date reflects the most recent version.
